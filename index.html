Orthopantomogram

An orthopantomogram (OPG), also known as a panoramic dental X-ray, is a comprehensive two-dimensional image of the entire mouth. It captures the teeth, jaws, temporomandibular joints (TMJs), and surrounding structures in a single image.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth Whitening is among the most popular cosmetic dental procedure, as it greatly improves the look of teeth. 'Whitening' is not a one-time procedure, it needs to be repeated time to time.

Full And Partial Dentures

A 'Denture' is a removable replacement for missing teeth and surrounding tissues. two types of dentures are available : Complete and Partial Dentures. Complete dentures are used when all teeth are missing while Partial dentures are given, where some natural teeth remain.
